Phishing Awareness training 

Rules for the training to look for : 

It's important to verify the actual email address instead of just the name.There are several mispelled words and a sense of urgency to urge you to click.

Often times, attackers will create a sense of urgency, talk about things that are too good to be true, flattery, mispellings, or other suspicious word usage.This is suspicious, notice how they misspelt words that are highlighted? .


Does this look like the company's official page? Often times, hackers will simply use an IP address like 169.054.233.119 instead of an actual URL.


When doing anything online that involves private matters like finances, passwords, etc., always ensure that the site is using 'https' and has a green lock.



The same rules apply; take things slow, check the sender's email address, hover over links before clicking, and check the website URL before inputting anything.
